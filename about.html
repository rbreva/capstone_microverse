The Tech Meet brings together the community of experts, academics and activists who comprise the Be Creative affiliate network in a different country every two years.
This year, we hope to expand our invitations list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the commons, free culture and open kwonledge.
So if you’re active and engaged in the world of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open Knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, goverments and foundations, lawyers, and activist - we hope you’ll consider joining us this year to build, a stronger, more vibrant commons together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about Tech Meet Congress 2022!
